Who we are

There are a lot of NGO’s and organizations provide basic needs for kids in need. We wanted to be a little different. Children need the right emotional support to be able to grow up confident, independent and healthy. For children who do not have parents uardians, loneliness and poverty can be worst enemies, pushing them to extreme steps. We bring in such kids and ensure support them, be their emotional fallback and help them feel better.
