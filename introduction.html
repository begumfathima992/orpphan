INTRODUCTION

Home >Introduction

The words ‘ASHA KUTEER’ means ‘the home of hope’. Children are our lifeline. They give us hope and we need to give back the same to them. We, from ASHA KUTEER, take it up as our responsibility to work for underprivileged children. We started off as a non-profit organization in January 2013 and right now have 45 abandoned and orphaned kids under our care. Based out of Hyderabad, we have two homes for boys and one home for girls all around the same locality.

While we aim at providing food, shelter, education and medical care to the kids, we imply more on giving them the emotional support they deserve. Hope is being able to see light at the end of the tunnel. We ensure our kids’ approach life with hope every day.
